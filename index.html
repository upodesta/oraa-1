About ORAA-85

Rajshahi University (RU) is one of the most prestigious public universities in Bangladesh, established in 1953. The Department of Accounting & Information Technology has produced numerous distinguished professionals.

ORAA-85 is an association of former students of the Accounting Department, Batch 1985.
